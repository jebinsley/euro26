Tips & Know Before You Go

🍺 Hacker-Festzelt Without a Reservation
Arrive at 10 AM when gates open — you'll almost always get a table on weekday mornings (Tue–Thu). The Sep 22–24 dates work in your favor. Afternoons, especially after 2 PM, are standing-room only. The beer garden seats are easier to grab than indoor seats.
🚄 Roma Termini vs. Tiburtina
The Nightjet departs Roma Tiburtina, not Termini. Take Metro Line B from Termini (~5 stops, 10 min, €1.50). Or take a taxi (~€10–15, 15 min). Plan to be at Tiburtina by 17:30 at the latest — trains in Italy run on time.
⛴️ Positano Departure Day
Sep 21 is your most logistically complex day: ferry + train + night train. Book the early morning ferry (7–8 AM) to give yourself a 3-hour buffer in Rome. Pack bags the night before — Positano streets are steep and narrow.
🎂 Birthday Celebration Ideas
Elizabeth's actual birthday — if it falls during the Amalfi stretch, a private sunset boat along the coast with Champagne is hard to beat. Or a table at Le Sirenuse's rooftop bar. In Munich, a private booth at Hacker-Festzelt would be the memorable moment.
